ABOUT ME

Crafting Visual Excellence

I'm a passionate media producer with over 3 years of experience in videography, photography, video editing, graphic design, and UX/UI. My expertise lies in capturing the essence of large-scale events, beauty pageants, and creating compelling content for television and digital platforms.

From filming major university events to collaborating with Vietnam Television (VTV9), I've honed my skills in working under pressure while maintaining the highest quality standards. My work combines technical precision with artistic vision to deliver content that resonates.

Whether it's shooting a high-profile beauty pageant, editing a corporate video, or designing a digital experience, I bring dedication, creativity, and professionalism to every project.
